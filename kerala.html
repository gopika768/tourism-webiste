KERALA

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a lush and serene state located in the southern part of India. It is renowned for its tranquil backwaters, picturesque hill stations, and vibrant cultural traditions. Visitors can explore the serene backwaters of Alleppey, the tea gardens of Munnar, the exotic wildlife in Periyar National Park, and the pristine beaches of Kovalam. Kerala is also famous for its Ayurvedic treatments, traditional Kathakali dance performances, and the unique cuisine that tantalizes taste buds with spices and flavors.
Price: A 5-day Kerala tour starts at approximately ₹12,000 per person, including accommodation, transport, and guided tours.

GUJARAT

Gujarat is a culturally rich state on the western coast of India, famous for its diverse heritage, vibrant festivals, and historical landmarks. From the stunning Rann of Kutch to the serene Gir National Park, home of the Asiatic lion, Gujarat offers a mix of natural beauty and cultural experiences. Popular attractions include the ancient city of Dwarka, the salt desert of Rann of Kutch, the picturesque Somnath Temple, and the stately Statue of Unity, the world’s tallest statue.
Price: A 5-day Gujarat tour starts at approximately ₹15,000 per person, covering accommodation, transport, and guided tours.

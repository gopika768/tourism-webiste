MAHARASHTRA

Maharashtra, a dynamic state in western India, is a blend of rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse landscapes. Known for its iconic landmarks, Maharashtra is home to the Gateway of India in Mumbai, the ancient rock-cut Ajanta and Ellora Caves, and the historic forts of Shivaji Maharaj. Visitors can explore the bustling city of Pune, the serene hill stations of Lonavala and Mahabaleshwar, and the pristine beaches of Alibaug and Ganpatipule. Maharashtra is also famous for its vibrant festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi, delectable Maharashtrian cuisine, and bustling nightlife in Mumbai.

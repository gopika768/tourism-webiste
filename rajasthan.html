RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan is a culturally rich state in northern India, celebrated for its royal heritage, colorful festivals, and architectural wonders. From the golden sands of the Thar Desert to the majestic forts of Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur, Rajasthan offers a unique blend of history and beauty. Popular attractions include the Amber Fort, Jaisalmer Fort, Pushkar's holy lake, and the serene Ranthambore National Park, home to the majestic Bengal tiger.
Price: A 5-day Rajasthan tour starts at approximately ₹18,000 per person, covering accommodation, transport, and guided tours.

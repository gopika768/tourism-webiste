DELHI

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a mesmerizing blend of historical landmarks, bustling bazaars, and modern attractions. From the majestic Red Fort and serene Lotus Temple to the vibrant Chandni Chowk and modern Connaught Place, Delhi offers a captivating journey through time and culture. Don’t miss the India Gate and the grandeur of Rashtrapati Bhavan.
Price: A 3-day Delhi tour starts at approximately ₹10,000 per person, including accommodation, local sightseeing, and transport.

HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal Pradesh, a picturesque northern state of India, is known for its stunning landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and lush valleys. Popular destinations include the serene town of Manali, the spiritual vibes of Dharamshala, and the colonial charm of Shimla. Adventure enthusiasts can indulge in trekking, paragliding, and skiing. Himachal is also home to breathtaking spots like Spiti Valley, Dalhousie, and Kasol. This state is a paradise for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.
Price: A 7-day Himachal tour starts at approximately ₹18,000 per person, including accommodation, transport, and sightseeing.

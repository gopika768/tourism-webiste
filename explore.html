KERALA

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a top tourist destination known for its serene backwaters, beautiful beaches, and lush hill stations. With its vibrant culture, Ayurveda treatments, and scenic landscapes, Kerala offers a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers.

HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal Pradesh is a scenic state in northern India, known for its stunning mountain landscapes, picturesque hill stations, and adventure sports. Popular destinations like Shimla, Manali, and Dharamshala offer a perfect mix of natural beauty, spiritual retreats, and outdoor activities like trekking, skiing.

DELHI

Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant city that blends rich history with modernity. From the iconic Red Fort and India Gate to bustling markets like Chandni Chowk, Delhi offers a unique cultural experience with a mix of ancient monuments, vibrant street food, and contemporary urban life.

GUJARATH

Gujarat, located on the western coast of India, is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and diverse landscapes. From the Rann of Kutch and the Gir National Park to the bustling city of Ahmedabad, Gujarat offers a unique mix of heritage, spirituality, and modern attractions.

RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings," is renowned for its magnificent forts, palaces, and desert landscapes. Discover the beauty of Jaipur's Amber Fort, Udaipur's lakes, and Jaisalmer's golden dunes.

EXPLORE
TAMIL NADU

Tamil Nadu, a southern state of India, is famous for its ancient temples, serene hill stations, and beautiful beaches. Visit Chennai, the cultural hub, and explore the temples of Madurai and Mahabalipuram.

EXPLORE
MAHARASHTRA

Maharashtra is known for its bustling cities, scenic hill stations, and historical forts. Explore Mumbai's Gateway of India, Pune's cultural heritage, and the caves of Ajanta and Ellora.

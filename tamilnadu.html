TAMIL NADU

Tamil Nadu, located in southern India, is a land of ancient temples, scenic landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Known as the "Land of Temples," it is home to architectural marvels like the Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur, the Shore Temple in Mahabalipuram, and the Meenakshi Temple in Madurai. Visitors can also explore the serene hill station of Ooty, the lush tea gardens of Coonoor, the pristine beaches of Kanyakumari, and the bustling city of Chennai. Tamil Nadu is famous for its classical music, Bharatanatyam dance, and delectable South Indian cuisine.
